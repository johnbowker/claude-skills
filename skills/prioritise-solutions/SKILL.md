# Prioritising Solutions & Assumption Mapping

Given a list of brainstormed solutions and product strategy context:

## Instructions

1. Rank the solutions in terms of likely customer impact
2. For the top 2, list the key assumptions that need to be tested
3. Suggest how to prototype or validate each solution quickly

### Context to Gather
- List of brainstormed solutions
- Strategy lens (customer segment, product goals)
- Resource constraints (lightweight testing preferred)

### Output Format
Prioritized shortlist with testable assumptions.

### Considerations
- Focus on uncertainty, not effort
- Assume nothing is "known" until validated

Ask the user for their solutions list and strategy context if not provided.
