# Refining Your Outcome Statement

Help improve and refine an outcome statement by:
- Making it specific and measurable
- Focusing on a change in user behavior or experience
- Aligning it to the organisation's mission to improve product activation and value delivery

## Instructions

Given the user's current outcome statement, return 3 improved outcome statements along with a 1-line rationale for each.

### Context to Gather
- Original outcome statement
- Target persona and product area (e.g. developer onboarding, small business tools)

### Output Format
Return 2-3 concise, actionable outcome statements with rationale.

### Considerations
- Avoid feature framing; focus on the user's success
- Link to known friction points from past research

Ask the user for their outcome statement if not provided.
