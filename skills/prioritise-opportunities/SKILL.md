# Prioritising Opportunities Based on Company Goals

Given a list of identified opportunities and the organisation's current strategy, goals, and OKRs:

## Instructions

1. Rank the top 3 opportunities by alignment to business impact
2. Flag any that might be misaligned or controversial
3. Recommend the one opportunity most likely to accelerate progress on the outcome

### Context to Gather
- Opportunity list
- Relevant OKRs, product themes, or known business levers

### Output Format
Ranked list with reasoning and a suggested focus area.

### Considerations
- Highlight internal tensions or misalignments as conversation starters
- Treat this as a prioritization hypothesis

Ask the user for their opportunity list and strategy context if not provided.
